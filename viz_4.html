Wind Speed

Wind speed is a fundamental atmospheric quantity caused by air moving from high to low pressure, usually due to changes in temperature. It seems as though most of the cities are located in an area with wind speed below 15mph. There is no city located in area with wind speed above 35mph. There seems to be no direct relationship between wind speed and latitude.
